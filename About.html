About Us

At J&O Aquafix, we are dedicated to providing reliable, fast, and high-quality plumbing solutions for homes and businesses. Our team of skilled professionals ensures that every installation, repair, or maintenance task meets the highest standards of safety and efficiency.

Our goal is to give every client peace of mind, knowing their plumbing system is in capable hands. From leaks and clogged drains to complex installations, J&O Aquafix combines experience, modern technology, and personalized service to deliver lasting results.

At J&O AquaFix, we believe that great service goes beyond fixing pipes. It’s about building trust with every customer. We pride ourselves on transparent communication, fair pricing, and treating every property with the utmost respect. As a locally owned and operated business, we’re proud to serve our community and remain available 24/7 for all your emergency plumbing needs.

What do we do:

At J&O Aquafix, we handle all things plumbing, providing quick and long-lasting solutions for your home or business. Whether it’s emergency repairs, routine maintenance, or new installations, our team delivers expert service you can trust. From fixing leaks and unclogging drains to installing state-of-the-art water heaters and eco-friendly fixtures, we combine experience and the latest technology to keep your plumbing system running smoothly. Customer satisfaction and quality workmanship are at the heart of everything we do, ensuring your peace of mind and comfort all year round.
